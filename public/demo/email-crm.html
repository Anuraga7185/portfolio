Cold Outreach

Sample Email: Initial Contact

Personalized approach to HR professionals

From: outreach@hired60.com
To: hr.director@companyxyz.com
Day 1 - Initial
Quick question about your hiring process at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] recently posted 5+ open roles on LinkedIn. Congrats on the growth!

I'm reaching out because we're building Hired60 - a platform that helps companies like yours discover pre-vetted candidates. We're currently gathering feedback from HR leaders before launch.

Quick question: What's your biggest challenge when sourcing quality candidates right now?

We'd love 15 minutes of your time to:

  • Share what we're building (with a quick demo)
  • Get your honest feedback on features
  • Learn about your current hiring pain points

In exchange, you'll get early access when we launch + your feedback will directly shape the product.

Book a 15-Min Call →

P.S. - We're already working with beta testers from [Similar Company 1] and [Similar Company 2]. Happy to share what they're saying!

Why This Worked:
  • Personalized with company-specific research (open roles)
  • Clear value exchange: feedback for early access
  • Low-commitment ask: 15 minutes, not a sales call
  • Social proof from similar companies
Follow-Up

Sample Email: Follow-Up

Persistence with added value

From: outreach@hired60.com
To: hr.director@companyxyz.com
Day 4 - Follow-up
Re: Quick question about your hiring process at [Company]

Hi [Name],

Following up on my message earlier this week - I know inboxes get busy!

I wanted to share something specific that might be relevant: We just added a feature based on feedback from [Similar Company] where candidates can showcase project portfolios, not just resumes.

Early beta testers told us this cut their screening time by 40% because they could assess real work upfront.

Still interested in giving feedback? We're only doing 10 more feedback sessions this month before we finalize features.

Grab a 15-Min Slot →

Not interested? No problem - just reply "not now" and I won't follow up again.

Follow-Up Strategy:
  • Added new value (specific feature update)
  • Included social proof with measurable result (40% time savings)
  • Created urgency (limited feedback slots)
  • Gave easy opt-out option to respect their time

What These Numbers Mean

  • 20% open rate is realistic for personalized cold outreach to HR professionals (industry average is 15-25%)
  • 210-300 meetings over 7-10 months = consistent pipeline of product feedback and potential customers
  • 5% meeting rate from opens shows quality targeting and compelling value proposition
  • Each meeting provided insights that shaped Hired60's features before launch
Key Learnings:
  • Personalization matters: Emails with company research had 2x higher reply rate
  • Tuesday-Thursday 10 AM sends performed best
  • Subject lines under 50 characters got more opens
  • Asking for feedback (not selling) lowered resistance
